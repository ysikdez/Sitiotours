Useful Information

Recommendations

If meals have not been requested during the booking process or arrive to the residence, the residents should provide food by themselves.

Bed sheets and towels are changed every week. Anyway, residents are responsible for mantaining clean all the spaces they use (bedrooms, studios, kitchen, etc.). Also, they should throw the garbage inside the garbage tins of the residence, after separating glass, plastic and organic waste from undifferenciated waste.

Weather

Cusco region presents itself with a dry and sunny weather, with low temperatures in the evenings between the months of June and August. The rainy seasons begin around December and finish at the end of March.

For any season we recommend: warm clothes, a raincoat, resistant shoes (boots or hiking shoes), t-shirts, a hat or a cap and sunscreen protection.

Nearby Services

Arin has several small shops but major articles can be purchased in the nearby towns of Calca and Urubamba, which are only minutes away by car.

At the residence, WI-FI connection is only available at the working spaces area. If you need a private connection, please let us know in advance to your residency.

For further information, please contact us: info@residenciakai.com
