LOCATION

Kai is located at Rosa Caballerayoc S/N Arin, Calca (Cusco – Peru).

HOW TO GET TO KAI

Regular flights arrive to Cusco city, everyday. From there you can take a taxi cab all the way to Arin, directly to the residence. This journey takes 1 hour aproximately.

If you are interested, Kai also can organize your pick-up from the airport or at any other point in Cusco. Contact us to get more information about this service.
